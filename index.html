Algiers Developer Meetup

Algiers Developers Meetup is a community-driven events about software, web and mobile technologies.

Meetups are held every month around a given topic related to programming.

Algiers Dev... Wait, what ?!

Community Driven

The subject of an event is of course chosen by the organizers, but it is up the community to propose the subjects to choose from.

You have a suggestion to make? Send it over to the organizers, they'll be happy to listen to you!

Date & Location ?

Meetups are held every month or so, and the location is at Djezzy Training Center, Bir Mourad Rais, Algiers.

Algiers Developer Meetup is organized under the umbrella of partnership between Djezzy & GDG Algiers.

The Meetups...

The meetups are about everything related to software development, engineering, web technologies and security.

It is where developers, engineers and geeks meet, share knowledge, eat, and just be themselves... Geeks!
